Help protect net neutrality in the EU

 edk on 2016-07-04

As many of you no doubt know, the European Union passed legislation last year intended to protect net neutrality. However, it contained significant loopholes which many people believe will allow European ISPs to prioritise paying partners' traffic. Among other things, this is potentially quite scary for FOSS projectsâ€”most of which don't have the kind of funding necessary to enter into such arrangements.
